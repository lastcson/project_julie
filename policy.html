Dog Darn Cute Grooming L.L.C. House Call Policy Agreement

Every client will be asked to read and sign the Policy Agreement as follows.

I, the client, hereby entrust Julie Scheerschmidt, Owner of Dog Darn Cute Grooming L.L.C., with the responsibility of grooming my pet(s) and understand the following:

Julie Scheerschmidt, the groomer, may be accompanied by her daughter, Vivian Scheerschmidt.

A bathroom tub, kitchen sink, or the like, will be needed to bathe my pet(s) in.
1. Grooming equipment and accessories will need to be set up in the designated area(s) before the grooming process begins. This may include seperate areas for bathing and drying.
2. The area(s) in which grooming will take place needs to be adequately cleaned for health and safety concerns. In the event the grooming area is not deemed adequately cleaned, the groomer has the right to reschedule and/or decline the groom.

The pet(s) being groomer may become restless during the grooming process.
1. This may result in accidental cuts, nicks, or the like, which the groomer will not be held liable for.

If the groomer finds evidence of fleas or ticks on my pet(s), I will be charged a $75 fee for de-bugging my pet(s), and deep cleaning of any used Dog Darn Cute Grooming L.L.C. tools.
1. The aforementioned fee will be in addition to the original grooming fee.
2. The pet(s) will be given Advantage or Frontline as a further deterrent.

The groomer may take photos or videos my pet(s) before, during, or after grooming for use on Dog Darn Cute Grooming L.L.C. social media.

Any and all health conditions pertaining to the pet(s) must be disclosed, along with any special care the pet(s) require.

Scheduled appointments may be cancelled by the groomer due to weather, sickness, road conditions, equipment failure, or the like. Effort by the groomer will be made to contact me in advance to reschedule, though not contact is not guarunteed.

Safety is priority during the grooming process, for humans and pets alike. The owner of the pet(s) must inform the groomer prior to grooming if the pet(s) has bitten someone or has aggressive tendencies. The owner will be held liable for any property damage caused by the pet(s).The use of a muzzle, e-collar, sling, straps, or the like, are acceptable safety measures and may be used by the groomer is deemed necessary. The groomer has the right to refuse service in the event the pet cannot be handled safely.

We request your permission to obtain vaccination records (rabies, distemper, parvovirus disease) to ensure all vaccinations are current.

I, the undersigned, do understand and agree to the above terms for the grooming and maintenance of my pet(s), and in consideration of the grooming services of Dog Darn Cute Grooming agree to hold harmless from damage, loss or claims arising from any known or unknown pre-existing condition of my pet(s).

I authorize Julie Scheerschmidt [Dog Darn Cute Grooming] to act as my agent in the event emergency veterinarian services, boarding, care-taking, and/or transportation is necessary and I agree to pay all costs. Any/all damages, loss or claim shall include but not limited to death, injury, or shock. Said pre-existing conditions shall not be limited to advanced age, extreme nervousness, neurosis, illness, previous injury, skin or coat conditions, or mental conditions.

I have read and understand my rights and obligations as written in this agreement for the services by Julie Scheerschmidt [Dog Darn Cute Grooming].
